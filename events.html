Events

A variety of events, from flower exhibitions to workshops are all over the nation for us to join and engage in. Find out the different events that suit your interests and timing. We also have our weekly or monthly discussions for members of the Club to interact and have a fruitful time together. An ongoing pandemic would not deter the club members from having hearty discussions as we have plenty of online meetings planned!
